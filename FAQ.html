Frequently Asked Questions

What distinguishes "graduate" from "undergraduate" degrees?

Following high school, undergraduate programs might result in a bachelor's degree (four years) or an associate's degree (two years). Following a bachelor's degree, graduate programs can lead to a master's or doctoral degree.

What is online learning?

When a student and an instructor are located in different locations, distance education is used. Learning can take place through the mail, phone, internet, or other channels.

What steps are involved in applying for a transfer?

You must meet the prerequisites for freshmen applicants as well as any extra requirements set forth by the transfer institution.
